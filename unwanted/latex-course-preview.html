Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience with LaTeX?
No prior experience is required. The course starts with the basics and gradually progresses to more advanced topics.
What software will I need?
We'll help you set up a free LaTeX distribution (TeX Live or MiKTeX) and a suitable editor. All required software is open-source.
Is this course suitable for my field of study?
Yes! LaTeX is widely used across all academic disciplines, especially in STEM fields, social sciences, and humanities.
