Google Earth Engine for Environmental Monitoring

Leverage the power of Google Earth Engine (GEE) for large-scale environmental monitoring, land cover change detection, and climate analysis. Gain hands-on experience with real-world datasets and cloud-based geospatial analysis.

Course Overview

This course introduces you to the Google Earth Engine platform and its applications in environmental monitoring. You will learn to access, process, and analyze satellite data in the cloud, and apply GEE to real-world environmental challenges.
